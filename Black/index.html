Black Sabbath

Black Sabbath is an English rock band that formed in Birmingham, England. They are often cited as being one of the pioneers of heavy metal. They have won 3 Grammys and released a staggering 19 albums. They are most famously known as the band that launched Ozzy Osbourne's career. After Ozzy went to pursue his solo career, Ronnie James Dio would take his spot, until eventually pursuing his own solo career.
