Iron Maiden

Iron Maiden is a classic metal band that formed in Leyton, East London in 1975. After a revolving door of band members was finally solidified in 1979, the band released their debut in 1980. Since then, the band has made 16 studio albums, and is still making music to this day. They have won 1 Grammy and have sold over 100 million albums worldwide, making them one of most comercially successful metal bands of all time.
