Judas Priest

Judas Priest is a classic heavy metal band that formed in 1969. They hail from West Bromwich, England. they are commonly cited as one of the greatest heavy metal acts of all time. Despite only winning one Grammy, they have sold over 50 million copies of their albums in the United States. Since 1974, they have released a whopping 18 albums and are still making music to this day. They are currently nominated to be inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
