Led Zeppelin

Led Zeppelin is a classic rock band that formed in 1968. Hailing from London, they are cited as being a progenitor to heavy metal and drew influences from blues and folk music. Since their tenure from 1969 to 1979, they released 8 studio albums and have won 7 Grammys. They are arguably one of the greatest rock bands of all time.
